Internet Service Providers in Canada (ISPs) offer various different packages.

The first thing to know is that residential packages for internet in Canada cost more than in many other countries.

Bell, Rogers, Telus, Virgin and Shaw are among the bigger ISPs in Canada offering packages. Availability will depend on your location. For instance, Bell and Rogers are the big internet service providers in Ontario, while Shaw and Telus are the main players competing in western provinces.
